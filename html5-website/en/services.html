All Services Are Advisory

We do not implement solutions, manage projects, or provide ongoing support. Our role is to provide the analysis and recommendations that enable your informed decision. Implementation is the responsibility of your team or selected vendors.

Vendor Neutrality & Introductions

NeoTechnology Solutions LLC provides decision consulting independently. We do not resell products as part of the advisory service.

If a client requests introductions to vendors or specialists, any referral fees are disclosed and documented in advance, and the client remains free to choose any provider. Read our Disclosure Policy
