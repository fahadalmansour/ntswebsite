Important information about the nature and limitations of our advisory services

Advisory Only: NeoTechnology Solutions provides analysis, recommendations, and decision frameworks. We do not implement solutions, guarantee outcomes, or make decisions on your behalf.

1. Nature of Our Services

NeoTechnology Solutions LLC provides IT consulting and advisory services. Our services consist of:

  • Analyzing your technology situation and requirements
  • Developing and presenting options for your consideration
  • Providing recommendations based on our analysis
  • Documenting findings in written deliverables

What We Are

  • Independent advisors providing analysis and recommendations
  • A source of structured information to support your decision-making
  • Consultants who help you understand options and trade-offs

What We Are Not

  • Implementation partners or systems integrators
  • Managed service providers
  • Product vendors or resellers
  • Legal, tax, or accounting advisors
  • Guarantors of business outcomes

2. No Guarantee of Outcomes

IMPORTANT: Technology decisions involve inherent uncertainty. We cannot and do not guarantee any specific outcomes, results, or return on investment from following our recommendations.

Our recommendations are based on information available at the time of analysis. Technology markets, vendor capabilities, regulatory requirements, and business conditions change. Past performance of recommended approaches or vendors does not guarantee future results.

Factors Outside Our Control

The success of any technology initiative depends on many factors that are outside the scope of our advisory services, including but not limited to:

  • Quality of implementation by your team or vendors
  • Organizational change management and adoption
  • Accuracy and completeness of information provided to us
  • Business and market conditions
  • Vendor performance and stability
  • Regulatory changes
  • Resource availability and commitment
  • Technical challenges discovered during implementation

3. Client Responsibility

By engaging our services, you acknowledge and agree that:

3.1 Decision Authority

You make all final decisions. Our recommendations are input for your decision-making process. The ultimate decision to proceed with any course of action, select any vendor, or implement any technology rests solely with you.

3.2 Information Accuracy

Our analysis depends on the information you provide. You are responsible for ensuring that the information you provide to us is accurate, complete, and current. Recommendations based on inaccurate or incomplete information may not be appropriate.

3.3 Independent Verification

You should independently verify critical information before making significant decisions. This includes:

  • Vendor claims and references
  • Cost estimates and pricing
  • Technical specifications and capabilities
  • Compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Contract terms and conditions

3.4 Professional Advice

Our services do not constitute legal, tax, accounting, or other professional advice. For matters requiring such expertise, you should consult qualified professionals in those fields.

4. Limitations of Analysis

4.1 Point-in-Time Analysis

Our analysis reflects conditions at the time of the engagement. Technology markets evolve rapidly. Vendor capabilities, pricing, market positions, and regulatory requirements may change after our deliverables are completed.

4.2 Information Constraints

Our analysis is limited to information that is reasonably accessible during the engagement period. We may not have access to all relevant information, and some information may be proprietary, confidential, or simply unknown.

4.3 Scope Boundaries

Our analysis addresses the specific questions and scope defined in our engagement agreement. Topics outside the defined scope are not addressed, even if they may be relevant to your broader technology decisions.

4.4 Assumptions

Our recommendations are based on assumptions that we document in our deliverables. If these assumptions prove incorrect, the recommendations may not be appropriate. You should review and validate assumptions before acting on recommendations.

5. Third-Party Information

Our analysis may incorporate information from third-party sources, including:

  • Vendor documentation and marketing materials
  • Industry analyst reports
  • Public regulatory guidance
  • Reference conversations with other organizations

We do not warrant the accuracy or completeness of third-party information. We make reasonable efforts to use reliable sources, but cannot independently verify all third-party claims.

6. No Implementation Services

We provide advisory services only. We do not:

  • Implement technology solutions
  • Write code or configure systems
  • Manage implementation projects
  • Provide ongoing IT support or operations
  • Act as your agent in vendor negotiations

Implementation of any recommendations is your responsibility, typically carried out by your internal team or vendors you select.

7. Website Content Disclaimer

The information on this website is provided for general informational purposes only. It is not intended as specific advice for your situation.

7.1 Not Professional Advice

Website content, including blog posts, case studies, and service descriptions, does not constitute professional advice. Each organization's situation is unique, and general information may not apply to your specific circumstances.

7.2 No Client Relationship

Viewing our website or reading our content does not create a client relationship. A client relationship exists only when formalized through a signed engagement agreement.

7.3 Currency of Information

We make reasonable efforts to keep website content current, but technology information can become outdated quickly. Always verify current conditions before making decisions.
