Structured Methodology for Technology Decisions

A predictable process that delivers clarity

1

Understand Context

We understand your business objectives, technical environment, regulatory requirements, and organizational constraints.

2

Develop Options

We develop 2-3 viable approaches, each with different trade-offs in cost, risk, timeline, and capabilities.

3

4

Decision Framework

Vendor Neutrality & Introductions

NeoTechnology Solutions LLC provides decision consulting independently. We do not resell products as part of the advisory service.

What We Don't Do

Clarity on scope ensures aligned expectations

✕

No Implementation

We don't execute projects, write code, or manage deployments. We advise; your team or vendors implement.

✕

No Managed Services

We don't provide ongoing IT support, monitoring, or operations. Our role ends with deliverable handover.

✕

No Product Sales

We don't sell, resell, or distribute technology products. No inventory, no licenses, no commissions.

No Outcome Guarantees
