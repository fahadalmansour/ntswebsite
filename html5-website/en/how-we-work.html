How We Work

A structured, predictable methodology that delivers clarity. Six steps from initial conversation to final handover.

Our 6-Step Advisory Process

Every engagement follows this methodology. No surprises, no scope creep.

1

Decision Session

90 minutes

A focused conversation to understand your situation, objectives, and constraints. We discuss the decision you're facing and determine if our advisory approach is appropriate.

What Happens:

  • You describe your situation and the decision you need to make
  • We ask clarifying questions about business context and technical environment
  • We discuss constraints: budget, timeline, regulatory requirements
  • We provide initial observations and assess engagement fit

Outcome:

  • Mutual understanding of the decision scope
  • Initial assessment of complexity and approach
  • Decision on whether to proceed with formal engagement

No commitment required. This session helps both parties determine fit.

2

Scoping & Proposal

2-3 business days

Based on the Decision Session, we prepare a formal proposal with clearly defined scope, deliverables, timeline, and pricing.

What We Define:

  • Specific questions to be answered
  • Information we'll need from your organization
  • Deliverables you'll receive
  • Timeline and milestones
  • Fixed fee with payment terms

Outcome:

  • Detailed proposal document
  • Consulting Services Agreement (if accepted)
  • Clear expectations for both parties

All engagements are fixed-scope and fixed-fee. No hourly billing surprises.

3

Context Gathering

1-2 weeks (typical)

We collect the information needed to analyze your situation thoroughly. This includes documentation review, stakeholder interviews, and technical assessment.

Activities:

  • Review existing documentation (architecture, contracts, policies)
  • Conduct stakeholder interviews (typically 4-8 sessions)
  • Assess current technical environment (as applicable)
  • Review regulatory and compliance requirements
  • Gather market and vendor information

What We Need From You:

  • Access to relevant documentation
  • Availability of key stakeholders for interviews
  • Technical environment access (read-only, as needed)
  • Timely responses to clarification requests

Your responsiveness directly affects timeline. We maintain a shared tracker.

4

Options Development

1-2 weeks (typical)

We develop 2-3 viable approaches that address your requirements. Each option is designed to be realistic and implementable, with different trade-off profiles.

For Each Option, We Define:

  • Approach description and rationale
  • Key components and vendors (if applicable)
  • Cost estimate range
  • Timeline estimate
  • Key risks and mitigation strategies
  • Prerequisites and dependencies

Why Multiple Options:

  • Rarely is there one "right" answer
  • Different organizations have different priorities
  • Options enable informed trade-off decisions
  • Transparency builds trust

We don't sell solutions. Options are based solely on your requirements.

5

Trade-off Analysis & Recommendation

3-5 business days

We analyze each option against your specific criteria and provide a clear recommendation with documented rationale.

Analysis Includes:

  • Side-by-side comparison matrix
  • Cost-benefit analysis for each option
  • Risk assessment and mitigation approaches
  • Alignment with stated business objectives
  • Compliance and regulatory considerations
  • Implementation complexity assessment

Our Recommendation:

  • Primary recommended option with clear rationale
  • Conditions under which alternatives may be preferable
  • Key assumptions underlying the recommendation
  • Questions only you can answer

You make the final decision. We provide analysis; you decide.

6

Presentation & Handover

Half day

We present findings to your leadership team, answer questions, and hand over all deliverables. After this, the engagement concludes.

Presentation Includes:

  • Executive summary of findings
  • Detailed walkthrough of options and recommendation
  • Q&A session with stakeholders
  • Discussion of implementation considerations

Deliverables You Receive:

  • Decision Summary Document (PDF)
  • Options Comparison Matrix
  • Requirements Documentation
  • Vendor Evaluation Framework (if applicable)
  • Implementation Guidance
  • Presentation slides

All deliverables are yours to keep. No ongoing dependency.

Typical Timeline

Most engagements complete within 4-8 weeks

Step Duration Your Time Required
1. Decision Session 90 minutes 90 minutes (key stakeholders)
2. Scoping & Proposal 2-3 days 1 hour (review and approval)
3. Context Gathering 1-2 weeks 4-8 hours (interviews, documentation)
4. Options Development 1-2 weeks 1-2 hours (clarification calls)
5. Trade-off Analysis 3-5 days Minimal
6. Presentation & Handover Half day Half day (leadership team)
Total 4-6 weeks typical 10-15 hours total

Timeline varies based on scope complexity and your organization's responsiveness. Complex engagements may take 6-8 weeks.

Your Role in the Process

Successful engagements require active client participation

👥

Stakeholder Access

We need access to the right people: decision-makers, technical leads, and subject matter experts. Timely interview scheduling is critical.

📄

Documentation

Existing documentation helps us understand your current state. Architecture diagrams, contracts, policies, and previous assessments.

🕑

Responsiveness

Questions will arise during analysis. Quick responses to clarification requests keep the engagement on track.

🔒

Technical Access

For some engagements, read-only access to systems or environments may be needed. We never require administrative access.

🎯

Decision Authority

Clear decision-making authority ensures recommendations can be acted upon. We need to know who makes the final call.

💬

Honest Feedback

If something isn't working or requirements change, tell us early. Mid-course corrections are easier than end-of-engagement surprises.

After the Engagement

Our role ends with deliverable handover. Here's what happens next:

What We Provide:

  • 30 days of clarification support (email-based)
  • One follow-up call if needed
  • All deliverables in editable formats

What We Don't Do:

  • Implementation or project management
  • Ongoing advisory retainers
  • Vendor introductions with commercial interest

Our independence is our value. Once we hand over deliverables, you own the decision and its execution.
