About Us

Independent IT decision consulting. No vendor affiliations. No product sales. Just analysis and recommendations.

Why Advisory Only?

Most technology consultants also sell implementation services. This creates inherent conflicts of interest.

When a consultant knows they'll earn revenue from implementing a solution, their recommendation may be influenced by which option generates more implementation work. When they have partnerships with specific vendors, they may favor those vendors even when alternatives might be better fits.

We chose a different model. We only provide advisory services. We don't implement solutions. We don't resell products. We don't earn referral fees from vendors.

This means our only incentive is accurate guidance. We succeed when you make an informed decision — regardless of which option you choose or who implements it.

The Conflict-Free Difference

Traditional Consultant

  • Earns from implementation projects
  • Has vendor partnerships with commissions
  • May recommend larger scope than needed
  • Incentive: More billable hours

Our Model

  • Fixed-fee advisory only
  • No vendor relationships or commissions
  • Scope defined upfront, no creep
  • Incentive: Your informed decision

Our Values

Principles that guide every engagement

⚖

Independence

No vendor affiliations, no product inventory, no implementation revenue. Our recommendations are based solely on your requirements and our analysis. We disclose any potential conflict.

📊

Methodology

Documented frameworks, not verbal opinions. Every recommendation is traceable to requirements and supported by analysis. You can see how we reached our conclusions.

🔎

Transparency

We state limitations upfront. If a question is outside our expertise, we say so. If assumptions drive our analysis, we document them. No hidden agendas.

🛡

Integrity

We tell you what we believe, not what you want to hear. If we think you're making a mistake, we'll say so — with respect, but clearly. We'd rather lose a project than mislead a client.

💡

Clarity

Complex technology decisions made understandable. Trade-offs explained in business language, not technical jargon. You should understand why, not just what.

📦

Completeness

Deliverables that stand on their own. After we leave, you should have everything needed to proceed. No dependency on us for implementation or interpretation.
